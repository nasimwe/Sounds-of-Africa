About Tems

Read Full Biography

Temilade Openiyi, known as Tems, is a Nigerian singer, songwriter, and producer born on June 11, 1995, in Lagos. She rose to fame with her feature on Wizkid's hit "Essence," which charted on the Billboard Hot 100, and later collaborated with global stars like Drake and Future. Tems won a Grammy in 2023 for her work on Future's "Wait for U" and made history as the first Nigerian to co-write an Oscar-nominated song, Rihanna's "Lift Me Up."
