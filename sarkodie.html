About Sarkodie

Read Full Biography

Sarkodie (born Michael Owusu Addo on July 10, 1988, in Tema, Ghana) is a renowned Ghanaian rapper and entrepreneur. Known for his fast-paced rap style and fluency in English and Twi, he gained fame with his debut album *Makye* in 2009. Sarkodie has released several successful albums, including *Rapperholic* and *Black Love*, earning numerous awards, such as BET and Ghana Music Awards. He is also an influential philanthropist, founding the *Sarkodie Foundation* to support education and healthcare in Ghana.
